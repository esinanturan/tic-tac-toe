Privacy Policy for Tic Tac Toe

Effective Date: April 1, 2025

Introduction

Thank you for choosing to play our Tic Tac Toe game. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to your data.

Information We Collect

Our Tic Tac Toe game collects minimal data to provide you with the best gaming experience:

  • Game Settings: We store your preferences such as grid size, win conditions, round settings, and other customizable options.
  • Player Names: If you choose to customize player names, these are stored locally on your device.
  • Game Statistics: Information about your game history, including scores, completion times, and game results for the leaderboard functionality.
  • Device Information: Basic information about your device to ensure proper functionality, such as screen size and operating system version.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the game functionality
  • Save your game preferences and settings
  • Display leaderboard statistics
  • Improve the game experience

Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device using the device's storage systems. We do not transmit your game data or personal information to external servers except where explicitly noted for specific features.

Third-Party Services

Our app may include standard development tools and services:

  • Development frameworks: We use standard development tools that help us deliver the app, which may collect anonymous usage statistics.
  • Analytics: We may use basic analytics to understand general app usage patterns to improve the game. This data is anonymized and does not identify you personally.

Ads and Monetization

Our game may display advertisements served by third-party advertising networks. These networks may collect and use information about your device (but not personal information) to provide relevant advertisements. You can find information about opt-out options in your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Our game does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game is suitable for all ages, and we design our data collection practices to comply with laws protecting children's privacy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data stored on your device by the game
  • Clear saved game data and statistics through the in-game options
  • Delete the app, which will remove all locally stored data

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date.
